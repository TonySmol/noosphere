How it works

Noosphere turns every thought into a semantic vector. As you type, it searches your own base and broadcasts an anonymous query to peers in your room. Matching shared thoughts come back as resonance.

Lanes

Local — strong matches already in your base.

World — strong matches shared by peers right now.

Serendipity — adjacent, not-quite-exact thoughts. Where surprise lives.
